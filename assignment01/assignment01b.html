GVK EMRI (Emergency Management and Research Institute)

GVK EMRI is a pioneer in Emergency Management Services in India. As a non-for-profit professional organization operating in the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode, GVK EMRI is the sole professional Emergency Service Provider in India today. GVK EMRI handles medical, police, and fire emergencies through the "108 Emergency service." This is a free service delivered through state-of-the-art emergency call response centers and has over 2600 ambulances across Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Uttarakhand, Goa, Chennai, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Assam, Meghalaya, and Madhya Pradesh. With the expansion of fleet and services set to spread across more states, GVK EMRI will have more than 10,000 ambulances covering over a billion people by 2011.
